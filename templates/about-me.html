Hello, My name is Nadia Hina! I like to find crafty solutions for as many problems I can. My first encounter with programming was in the 6th grade. My 6th grade teacher, Mr.Contreas, held an afterschool robotics club that I joined. During club time he would create obstacle courses for us, outlining the path that we were supposed to follow with duct tape. I remember writing the code and running it on the robot and each time it took the course it would take a slightly different route, even though I hadn't changed the code. This would encourage me make tweaks in the code each run with no success. That is until I realized that where we put the robot as the starting position was of utmost importance! Once the starting position was consistent, things approved.

Again in middle school, I helped put together a part of a robot. In high school I joined FIRST Robotics and was fortunate enough to attend competitions in Davis, CA and San Jose, CA in 2022. I saw and participated in the building and coding of a robot.
